# 🍇 Cerrado Seco

> *Little internet space for my father wine computer needs*

My father has a small vineyard nestled in **Fervença, Celorico de Basto**, in the heart of the Minho region of northern Portugal. Every bottle of **Cerrado Seco** is the result of traditional, artisanal winemaking, no industrial shortcuts, no mass production. Just grapes, time, and love.

This repository hosts the official **nutritional information pages** for all Cerrado Seco wines, compliant with EU food labelling regulations.

## 🏷️ About the Labels

All nutritional information pages are generated to comply with **EU Regulation 2021/2117**, which requires alcoholic beverages sold in the EU to display full ingredient and nutritional declarations.

Each page is linked via a **QR code** on the wine's physical label, making the information accessible to anyone with a phone — no app required.

**Ingredients (all wines):** Grapes, preservatives (sulphites). Bottled or packaged under protective atmosphere.
